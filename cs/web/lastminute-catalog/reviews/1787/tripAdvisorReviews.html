clean and convenient

After spending all day in the Philadelphia airport after many cancelled flights we finally got a late flight to Raleigh. Upon arrival we called many hotels and this was the only one who still had a shuttle working, it was 11pm and we had a child with us. The driver said 10-15 minutes but it was more like 4 minutes! She was so nice and friendly and even checked us in when we arrived. The room was very clean and super comfy beds, so welcome after 8 hrs in an airport! The next day the driver was kind enough to bring us to Hertz to rent a car. All in all a great stay.
